drop the mp3/lrc files to the exe ico and then play it, use mouse or key to control it.

ESCAPE: exit the player.
SPACE: pause the player.

UP: louder sound.
DOWN: litter sound.

RIGHT: next music.
LEFT: back to front music.

Ctrl+UP: restart the music.
Ctrl+RIGHT: forward
Ctrl+LEFT: back

any keydown make the info of the music display on the consle.
